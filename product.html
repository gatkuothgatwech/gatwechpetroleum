PETROLEUM PRODUCTS

Petroleum products are derived from crude oil through various refining processes. Crude oil is a naturally occurring fossil fuel composed of hydrocarbon deposits, and refining transforms it into a range of useful products.

PETROL

Petrol is highly flammable, and safety precautions must be taken when handling, storing, and using it. It should be stored in approved containers and kept away from heat sources and open flames.

KEROSENE

Kerosene is commonly used as a fuel for heating homes, especially in portable heaters and in areas where natural gas or electricity is not readily available.
